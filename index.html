Tesla Model 3

By Fionn Tynan and Dara Cullen

This is the Tesla Model 2 changing to the Tesla Model 3

We are focusing on Intels job in creating the car



It was code named "BlueStar" in 2007 in the original business plan


The car costs about €35000


The car can go 0-60(0-97 km) in 5.6 seconds. The cars top speed in 130 mph(210 kmph)


Tesla’s operating system is built on a modified version of Linux Ubuntu. Musk says that Tesla aims to upgrade the Linux OS to 4.4 in Tesla vehicles in December. That’s when he expects they will be able to update the browser.


The Tesla Model 3 has 18 inch wheels.


The car is fitted with full self-driving hard-ware.


Keycard/bluetooth smartphone connection for vehicle access.


Intel

Intel are creating the infotainment system for the car


The electric car maker will no longer use chips from Nvidia to power its massive infotainment console, Bloomberg's Ian King first reported. Instead, Tesla will use chips supplied by Intel, the world's largest chipmaker.


The Model 3 and new versions of the Model X and Model S will rely on the Intel chips, according to the report.
